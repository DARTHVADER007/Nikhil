College FAQ's

Forgot my college mail password and I'm not able to change it on my own as I do with my normal Gmail...what should I do?
You need to visit your college admin who maintains all the student mails to reset your password.
What are the different labs in CSE Dept?
Our college comprises a total of 4 labs in the CSE department: 1) Advanced Computing Lab 2) DT Lab 3) INC Lab and lastly 4) Smart Coder's Lab.
Is NSRIT Accredited with NAAC?
Yes, NSRIT is accredited with NAAC "A" Grade with a CGPA of 3.10/4.00 and is also having NBA accreditation.
How can I check my result?
You can check your results by visiting the official college website "http://www.nsrit.edu.in/"...you can also pay fees, know your attendance and also know what is happening in your college.
Is NSRIT Autonomous?
Yes, NSRIT is autonomous and is affiliated with JNTU-GV.
